What Is a Typing Test?

A typing test is an online tool used to measure how fast and accurately a person can type. Typing speed is usually measured in WPM (words per minute).

During a typing test, users type a short text paragraph within a certain time limit. The system calculates typing speed, accuracy, and error rate.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

The average typing speed is around 40 words per minute.

Professional typists often reach 65 to 75 WPM, while advanced typists may exceed 100 WPM.

How to Improve Typing Speed

  • Practice typing every day
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Use correct finger placement
  • Increase typing test difficulty gradually

Typing Speed Chart (WPM)

Typing speed is measured in words per minute (WPM). The chart below shows typical typing speeds from beginner to professional typists.

Typing Level Words Per Minute (WPM) Description
Beginner 0 – 20 WPM New typists who are still learning keyboard layout.
Average 30 – 40 WPM Typical typing speed for many computer users.
Good 40 – 60 WPM Comfortable typing speed for work and productivity.
Fast 60 – 80 WPM Experienced typists with good accuracy.
Professional 80 – 100 WPM Advanced typists such as transcriptionists and programmers.
Expert 100+ WPM Elite typists who type extremely fast.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

A good typing speed depends on your goals. Many office jobs expect employees to type around 40 to 60 words per minute. Professional typists often exceed 80 WPM with high accuracy.

How to Improve Typing Speed

Improving typing speed requires consistent practice and good technique. Focus on accuracy first, use correct finger positioning, and practice with timed typing tests regularly.

Typing Test Results

After completing a typing speed test, you receive several important metrics that help you understand your typing performance.

Words Per Minute (WPM)

Words per minute (WPM) is the most common way to measure typing speed. One word is typically counted as five characters including spaces.

The higher your WPM, the faster your typing speed.

Typing Accuracy

Accuracy measures how many characters you typed correctly. High accuracy is essential for professional typing.

Most experienced typists aim for accuracy above 95%.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

The average typing speed is around 40 WPM. Professional typists often reach speeds of 65 to 75 WPM or more.

With daily practice, many people can significantly increase their typing speed and accuracy.

Practice Your Typing Speed

You can improve your typing performance by practicing regularly with typing tests and exercises.

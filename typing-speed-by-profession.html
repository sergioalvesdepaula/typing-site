Typing Speed by Profession

Typing speed requirements vary depending on the profession. Some jobs require only average typing ability, while others depend heavily on fast and accurate typing.

Profession Average WPM
Student 30–40 WPM
Office Worker 40–50 WPM
Programmer 50–70 WPM
Writer / Journalist 60–80 WPM
Professional Typist 80–100+ WPM

Why Typing Speed Matters at Work

Typing speed affects productivity, communication, and efficiency. Faster typing helps workers complete tasks more quickly, especially in jobs that involve heavy computer use.

Can You Improve Typing Speed?

Yes. Most people can improve their typing speed through regular practice, typing tests, and better keyboard technique.

Typing Speed Levels Explained

Typing speeds are generally categorized as:

  • Beginner: 20–30 WPM
  • Intermediate: 30–50 WPM
  • Advanced: 50–70 WPM
  • Expert: 70+ WPM

Average Typing Speed by Profession

  • Data Entry Clerk: 50–70 WPM
  • Programmer/Developer: 40–60 WPM
  • Writer/Journalist: 50–65 WPM
  • Student: 35–55 WPM

Best Exercises to Improve Typing Speed

  • Touch typing drills
  • Daily short typing tests
  • Typing games and challenges
  • Accuracy-focused exercises

Touch Typing vs Hunt and Peck

Touch typing is faster and more efficient than hunting and pecking. Learning correct finger placement and posture can dramatically improve your speed and accuracy.

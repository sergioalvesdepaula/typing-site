What Is a Good Typing Speed?

An average typing speed is around 40 WPM. Many experienced typists reach 60 WPM or more, while advanced typists can go much higher with strong accuracy.

How to Improve Typing Speed

  • Practice a little every day
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Use proper finger positioning
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Take tests with different time lengths

Why Take a 2 Minute Typing Test?

A 2 minute typing test gives a more stable measurement than shorter tests because it reduces luck and better reflects real typing endurance. It is useful for students, office workers, writers, and anyone who wants to improve keyboard skills.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

An average typing speed is around 40 words per minute. A good typing speed is often between 50 and 70 WPM, while advanced typists may go much higher with strong accuracy.

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice a little every day
  • Focus on accuracy first
  • Keep your hands relaxed
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Use correct finger placement

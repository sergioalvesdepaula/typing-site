What Is a Good Typing Speed?

An average typing speed is around 40 words per minute. Professional typists often reach speeds of 65–75 WPM or more. With regular practice, most people can significantly improve their typing speed and accuracy.

How to Improve Your Typing Speed

  • Practice typing daily
  • Focus on accuracy before speed
  • Keep your hands relaxed
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard
  • Use proper finger positioning

Why Take a 1 Minute Typing Test?

The one minute typing test is widely used because it provides a quick and reliable estimate of typing speed. It is commonly used by students, writers, programmers, and professionals who want to improve productivity.

Website Purpose

Free Typing Practice is an educational typing tool designed to help users improve keyboard skills. The site offers multiple typing test durations, typing practice paragraphs, typing certificates, and educational resources explaining typing speed and accuracy.

Our goal is to provide a clean and distraction-free environment where users can track their typing progress and develop faster typing habits.

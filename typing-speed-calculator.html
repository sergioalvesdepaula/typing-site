How Is Typing Speed Calculated?

Typing speed is usually measured in words per minute (WPM). One word is generally counted as five characters, including spaces and punctuation.

Gross WPM is calculated from the total number of characters typed. Net WPM is calculated after subtracting the impact of typing errors.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

A normal typing speed for many users is around 40 WPM. A good typing speed is often considered between 40 and 60 WPM, while advanced typists may type much faster with high accuracy.

Typing practice and regular typing tests are the best ways to improve typing speed over time.

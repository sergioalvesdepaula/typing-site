Typing Speed by Age

Typing speed can vary depending on age, computer experience, and daily practice. Younger users may still be learning keyboard positions, while adults with more experience often type faster.

Age Group Average WPM
Children (10–12) 15–25 WPM
Teenagers 30–40 WPM
Adults 40–50 WPM
Experienced Typists 60+ WPM

What Is a Good Typing Speed for Your Age?

A good typing speed depends more on practice than age. With regular practice, both teenagers and adults can improve significantly.

How to Improve at Any Age

Daily typing exercises, touch typing, and timed typing tests are useful for all age groups. The key is consistent practice and focusing on accuracy first.

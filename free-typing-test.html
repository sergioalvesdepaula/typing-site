How the Free Typing Test Works

During a typing test, users type a short paragraph or sentence within a specific time limit. The system calculates typing speed in words per minute (WPM) and tracks typing accuracy.

Typing tests usually include different time durations such as 1 minute, 2 minutes, or longer tests to evaluate typing endurance and consistency.

What Is a Good Typing Speed?

An average typing speed is around 40 WPM. Professional typists often reach 65–75 WPM or higher with high accuracy.

Regular typing practice helps improve both typing speed and accuracy over time.
